inGrid Demo

inGrid is a simple tool to help align your text to a baseline grid. Enter your line height and offset in pixels in the inGrid control box and click “inGrid it” to overlay a baseline grid in red. You can also install inGrid as a bookmarklet and summon it on any page — read the blog post for full details.
